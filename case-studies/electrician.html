Case Study — Electrician Lead Gen

VOLTAIC Electricians

A high-conversion lead generation website engineered to turn local search traffic into booked jobs. Built specifically for tradesmen who need a reliable, 24/7 online presence to capture customers searching for emergency services.

Our Role

Lead Designer & Developer

Timeline

8 Days

Tech Stack

HTML5, CSS3, Vanilla JS

Industry

Trades / Home Services

Focus

Lead Generation

The Challenge

The client was an experienced licensed electrician with years of happy customers, but zero online presence. All leads came through word-of-mouth, which is unreliable and unscalable. When anyone in their service area Googled "electrician near me," they didn't exist. They were losing jobs to competitors with mediocre websites just because they showed up online.

The Solution

We built a focused, trust-first lead generation website. Every design decision was driven by one goal: get the visitor to click "Request a Quote." The blue-and-dark color scheme communicates professionalism and reliability. Key trust signals — licensing badges, testimonials, service guarantees — are placed above the fold.

The quote form was kept short and friction-free by design: name, service type, phone number. That's it. The simpler the form, the higher the completion rate. Integrated with real-time notifications so we can respond instantly the moment someone submits.

Key Features Delivered

  • Conversion-optimized quote request form
  • 4-service grid with icon-based layout
  • Testimonial carousel with real reviews
  • Trust badge section (Licensed & Insured, 24/7)
  • Local SEO meta tags & Google Business schema
