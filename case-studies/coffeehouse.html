Case Study — Coffee House

The Velvet Roast

A luxury coffee house website featuring an elegant digital menu and visual storytelling that transforms a local café into a destination brand.

Our Role

Lead Designer & Developer

Timeline

12 Days

Tech Stack

HTML5, CSS3, Vanilla JS

Industry

Food & Beverage / Hospitality

Focus

Digital Menu & Reservations

The Challenge

The Velvet Roast is a high-end artisan coffee house that was being underperformed online by its own physical experience. They had a basic Google listing and nothing else — no website, no digital menu, no way to book a table online. Customers looking them up on their phones were bouncing immediately.

The Solution

We built a rich, dark-mooded website that perfectly captured the warmth and luxury of walking into their store. The design uses warm gold tones against deep charcoal backgrounds, with high-quality photography integration and smooth scroll-driven animations to make every section feel like turning a page in a modern magazine.

The digital menu was designed with elegance in mind — everything is scannable, beautiful, and instantly readable on a phone screen. The simple table reservation form integrates directly into our backend tools so you never miss a party.

Key Features Delivered

  • Interactive digital menu (Coffee & Food)
  • "Book a Table" form with confirmation email
  • Featured Blends / merchandise section
  • Premium photographic hero section
  • Fully mobile responsive with elegant typography
